My Top 5 Favorite Instruments

I love music and I highly doubt that I am the only one who does. Music is a very special thing. It reaches a part of our soul that not many other things can reach. We know this because even back when there were no special technology and tools, humans were creating musical instruments. This goes to show the importance of music in human life. With that being said, musical instruments can come in many forms.
Here are my favorites.

1. Saxophone

The saxophone is beautiful. That's pretty much all I can say about it. There is just something about it...something melodic. It just wooes your soul and leaves you in an angelic trance.

2. Piano

The piano is the most well known and most used musical instrument by far.
